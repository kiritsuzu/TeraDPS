TeraDPS Contact Information

There are several ways to contact and communicate with TeraDPS, most of our users use our social media outlets to talk about it, however, we also have an option to contact us directly by email.

Contact us directly by email

If you have any specific inquiries for TeraDPS, you may contact us at our main email: postmaster@email.teradps.io, for most inquiries, you can expect a reply back within 1-3 business days.

Chat in real-time on the TeraDPS Discord

TeraDPS has a public Discord channel that is available to anyone who wishes to chat about TeraDPS.io and its participating DPS meters (ShinraMeter & CasualMeter). You can visit our Discord by following this invitation link: https://discord.gg/0wjLnPs6HoNFxv6O, or by clicking on the Discord button on our home page.

Join the discussion at the official TeraDPS forums

TeraDPS uses Discourse to host their forums. If you are interested in discussing TeraDPS, you may sign-up for our official forums at http://forums.teradps.io/, it is free to sign-up and post.
