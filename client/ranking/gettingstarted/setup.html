CasualMeter Setup Guide

This guide will explain how to setup CasualMeter to upload to TeraDPS.

STEP 1: Download & Install CasualMeter

Visit CasualMeter on GitHub for instructions on downloading the meter. Alternatively, you can download it directly by clicking here.

STEP 2: Open CasualMeter Settings Directory


With CasualMeter open, right-click it in the system tray and select the Settings Directory option. This will open the settings folder for CasualMeter.
After successfully opening the folder, please close CasualMeter.

STEP 3: Open the Settings file


Navigate to the settings.json file in your Settings folder and open it with any word processor of your choice. Notepad is a good example.
Scroll down to the section with TeraDpsUser & TeraDpsToken. They should be empty, like the picture above.

STEP 4: Retrieve & Enter your Meter Info


Make sure you are registered and logged into TeraDPS.io before proceeding. Click "Get Meter Info" in the top-right corner of TeraDPS.io, and type in your account information to retrieve your UserId and AuthToken.
Enter these two codes into your settings file between the quotes, like the picture above.
Make sure CasualMeter is closed while editting your settings file, or your information will not be saved correctly!

STEP 5: Turn on exporting to TeraDPS in CasualMeter


Run CasualMeter and make sure Autoexport to TeraDPS.io is checked.

You're all done!

You're ready to start uploading to TeraDPS! CasualMeter will automatically upload any successful encounter you have upon killing a boss. Please remember that there are certain requirements for uploading to TeraDPS. Please see our Requirements Page to view submission requirements.

To avoid bugged encounters, we recommend only having one meter uploading for each party!
Please collaborate with your party to ensure only one person is uploading to avoid any bugs with your encounters.

ShinraMeter Setup Guide

This guide will explain how to setup ShinraMeter for uploading to TeraDPS.

STEP 1: Download & Install ShinraMeter

Visit ShinraMeter on GitHub for instructions on downloading the meter. Alternatively, you can download it directly by clicking here.

STEP 2: Open your window.xml file


Navigate to your window.xml file inside your ShinraMeter folder. It is located in /resources/config/ inside the main folder. Open the window.xml file with any word processor, like Notepad.
You will see an area with teradps.io and blank spaces inbetween user & token. This is where you will input your TeraDPS account information.

STEP 3: Retrieve & Input your Meter Info


Make sure you are registered and logged into TeraDPS.io before proceeding. Click "Get Meter Info" in the top-right corner of TeraDPS.io, and type in your account information to retrieve your UserId and AuthToken.
Enter these two codes between the user and token sections, like the picture above.
Make sure ShinraMeter is closed while editting your window.xml file, or your information will not be saved correctly!

STEP 4: Turn on exporting to TeraDPS in ShinraMeter


Run ShinraMeter and make sure Site Export is checked.

You're all done!

You're ready to start uploading to TeraDPS! ShinraMeter will automatically upload any successful encounter you have upon killing a boss. Please remember that there are certain requirements for uploading to TeraDPS. Please see our Requirements Page to view submission requirements.

To avoid bugged encounters, we recommend only having one meter uploading for each party!
Please collaborate with your party to ensure only one person is uploading to avoid any bugs with your encounters.
