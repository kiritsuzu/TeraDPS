What is the Top Ranker system?

The Top Ranker system was created to allow exceptionally skilled players to represent themselves on TeraDPS by leaving a lasting impression with top ranks. Due to TeraDPS expiring all non-premium records, there was no way to save encounter data for players who could not afford premium subscriptions.

Top ranks reward players for reaching record-level DPS scores by giving them permanent spots on the rankings, until they are 'beaten' by another record-level score. With this system in place, even players who are unable to afford subscriptions to TeraDPS can display their skill by achieving top ranks for their specific class/region on specific bosses.

How do top ranks work? How does placement work?

The top ranker system stores a limited number of rankings permanently on the server based on the very top scoring entries.

There are only 5 top rank spots available per boss, per class, per region!

When an exceptionally high score is uploaded to TeraDPS, it will have a chance to be added as a top rank. However, it must be higher than one of the scores in its current top rank bracket. If it is higher than another top rank score in its bracket, it will take the place of said score. As a result, the defeated score will be deleted from the rankings, while the new score will be added as a permanent top rank score.

Please understand that while top ranks CAN be permanent, if they are beaten by a new score, they will be removed.

Only one entry per player per ranking is allowed.

Players cannot have more than one spot on each ranking at any given time. This is to prevent monopolizing the rankings by a single player, and to give lower skilled players a chance at second/third/fourth/fifth place. If a player achieves a spot in the top rankings, then uploads another score for the same boss bracket, there are two scenarios:

1. If the player's next score has a DPS rating that is higher than his/her previous top rank record, his spot will be updated with his new score.

2. If the player's next score has a DPS rating that is lower than his/her previous top rank record, the entry will be placed as a normal expiring non-top-rank entry, regardless of if it is eligible for the top ranks.

We appreciate your understanding in this matter, and hope to present more opportunities for skilled players to make it onto the top rankings.
