Submission Guidelines & Requirements

TeraDPS is a great tool for tracking your encounters, since they will be saved on the site. However, not all encounters can be saved due to limited database storage, so there are a few requirements that must be fulfilled to have an encounter successfully approved. Without further adue, here are they are!

To avoid bugged encounters, we recommend only having one meter uploading for each party!
Please collaborate with your party to ensure only one person is uploading to avoid any bugs with your encounters.

Banned Consumables

There are a few consumable items and special event buffs that increase DPS by absurd amounts to the point where it would be unfair to count them alongside the rankings with normal, every-day accessible consumables. Here is the list of rejected consumables:

If these consumables or buffs are detected on anyone in your party when your encounter is submitted, it will be rejected! Please take note and consider your choice on what consumables you use.
  • TERA TIME (Ultra Rapid Fire) Buff:
  • Grants 80% cooldown reduction and massive mana regeneration.
  • Emblem Of Eminence:
  • Increases maximum stamina by 15. Prevents stamina decay. Recovers 1% of total HP every second. Reduces cooldown of all skills by 20%. Recovers 5% of total MP every second.
  • Glorious Memento:
  • Increases total HP by 7%. Increases endurance by 20%. Increases attack speed by 15%. Increases skill damage by 20%. Increases movement speed while in combat by 10. Prevents crystals from breaking.
  • Seal Of The Undying Meme:
  • Increases total HP by 7%. Increases endurance by 20%. Increases movement speed in combat by 10. Increases attack speed by 15%. Increases maximum stamina by 10. Recovers 1% of total HP every second. Increases skill damage by 20%. Prevents stamina decay. Prevents crystals from breaking.

Accepted Bosses

Please take note that only a certain range of bosses are accepted. This boss circuit will change with the flow of TERA as we move from patch to patch. To view the list of currently accepted bosses, please look at the "Dungeons" section of TeraDPS. If a boss is not listed in this section, it is currently not being accepted on TeraDPS, and encounters with said boss will not be uploaded to the site.

And that's it! We look forward to seeing you on the rankings, good luck and have fun!
